About Me

I am currently a PhD student in the Data Science and Operations department at the USC Marshall School of Business. I am advised by Vishal Gupta and Paat Rusmevichientong in the Operations Management group. My research interests are in developing better optimization methods and algorithms to improve decision making under uncertainty. I am interested in applications in healthcare, transportation, matching, and recommender systems. Previously, I obtained by M.S. and B.S. in Operations Research at Columbia University.
Interests. Data-Driven Optimization, Machine Learning
